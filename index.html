At Aluminium ONE, we continuously strive to adapt evolving technological advancements to extrusion machines and plants in the most effective and efficient way. With our R&D infrastructure and experienced workforce, we develop projects to provide improvement services in the areas of hardware and software. The main projects are the modernization of Quench, Puller, and Extrusion Presses. These services aim to maintain standard quality in production and optimize the lifespan of machines and equipment.
